I remember when Earth was created,

Earth was just a little asteroid.

We put planets together

"Holding hands"

I see everyone! Everyone feels me too!

A little girl shouts:

"Hi Everyone!
Hi Everyone!
Hi Everyone!
I see you
I love you!
I feel you!"

She feels the fullness in everything.
She feels incredibly alive.

She hears all the animals too, all at the same time from miles away; the crickets, a deer stepping into the crunchy leaves.

A vibration, a melody made by nature all together.

Everyone dances back to me, waving their bodies and branches and leaves.

"Now I will never unsee you," says the little girl.
She hugs everyone.

! Bye Everyone !
Echoes in the woods.
